WHO WE ARE

Istanbul Barbers is a Traditional Turkish Barbershop located on The Strand, Longton.

Offering authentic Turkish male grooming services such as Wet Shave, Hot Towel, Facial Hair Waxing and Ear Hair Flaming, as well as Haircutting and Styling.

A haircut from a Turkish Barber will never be rushed and will be finished to perfection.

Istanbul Barbers welcomes everyone to come and relax and enjoy a true Turkish experience!

High quality haircut and styling

Free hot towel, eyebrow trimming and ear hair flaming

Free wet shave every 6th visit
